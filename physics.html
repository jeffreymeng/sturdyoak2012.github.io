One of my ongoing projects is translating the Chinese Physics Olympiad problems, available online but only in Chinese. I translate the problems and post them on AoPS. Note: solutions posted by other people on the forums are not necessarily (and often not) correct. The solution that I (sturdyoak2012) post will be the correct one. (I confirm with the Chinese solutions before posting my English solution. Many problems are horribly complicated, so I do not post solutions to everything.) Here's my progress:
  • 1999:
    • Problem 1 (vapor pressure)
    • Problem 2 (lens system)
    • Problem 3 (superconductive loop)
    • Problem 4 (dark matter in binary system)
    • Problem 5 (loop's effective resistance)
    • Problem 6 (magnetic field parabolic path)
  • 2018:
    • Problem 1 (free fall)
    • Problem 2 (spring mass with friction)
    • Problem 3 (collision with rod)
    • Problem 4 (Ioffe-Pritchard magnetic trap)
    • Problem 5 (Zeeman effect)
    • Problem 6 (thermal radiation device)
